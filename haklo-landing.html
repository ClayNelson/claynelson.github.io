The problem we're solving

"Families don't drift apart because they stop loving each other. They drift apart because nothing holds the center."

How it works

01

The Ritual

Set a weekly gathering time. Every member receives a quiet prompt — a question worth sitting with. The gathering begins when someone arrives.

02

The Presence

Present members check in. Away members are honored, not erased. A family of five with one traveling is still a family of five — the circle holds.

03

The Memory

Decisions, reflections, and intentions are preserved — not as a log, but as a record of what this family chose and why. Something to return to.

The gathering card

Every gathering has a shape. This is yours.

The gathering card is the center of the Haklo experience. It holds the prompt, the members, the moment. It knows who is present and who is away — and it treats both with equal care.
